Flute Care Tips

🧴

Oiling the Bamboo

Once a month, oil the inside and outside with mustard oil. This helps prevent the bamboo from drying out and cracking.

🌡️

Temperature Control

Protect the flute from excessive heat and direct air from an AC. Extreme temperatures can affect the bamboo tuning.

🧤

Cleaning After Play

After playing, wipe away the moisture created by blowing into the flute with a clean cloth. This helps keep the flute fresh for a long time.
